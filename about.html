ABOUT US

Pine City Mall is the largest single-phase shopping mall to be built in Africa, but smaller than Gateway, Sandton, Menlyn and Fourways Mall. The total retail area is 131 000 square metres, and a gross area of 550 000 square metres

Expansive mall with brand-name fashion & footwear retailers, casual eateries & a movie theater.

Treat your self. Imagine a shopper’s paradise with over 300 local and international flagship stores, more than 25 restaurants, movie theatres and regular art exhibitions and fashion collectives. A place that heroes all things Africa; where local designers are showcased, with a start-up hub for new trending brands. Welcome to the largest single-phase mall in Africa, with easy access, ample parking, wide walkways and lots of natural light. And with South African firsts like Zara Home and Armani Exchange, as well as fashion favourites like Maxhosa, H&M and Versace—it’s a dream come true!
