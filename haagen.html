Since 1961 Häagen-Dazs has been synonymous with superior quality ice cream and offers over 24 flavours, made from the finest hand-selected ingredients sourced from around the world. Every flavour from the House of Häagen-Dazs starts with 100% real cream, milk, free-range eggs, and sugar – simple ingredients for a creamy, intense, enchanting ice cream. One of the most popular global ice cream brands today, Häagen-Dazs is available through 950 Häagen-Dazs Shops in more than 80 countries.
